# BRD Template

Use this structure for the descriptive handoff document.

## Business Requirements Document

### 1. Overview

- Campaign name
- Internal code
- Owner
- Date
- Version

### 2. Business Context

- What is happening
- Why this campaign is needed now
- Related initiative, launch, or commercial event

### 3. Objective and Success Measures

- Primary objective
- Secondary objective
- KPIs
- Hypothesis

### 4. Audience and Eligibility

- Primary audience
- Inclusion criteria
- Exclusion criteria
- Suppression rules
- Control group approach

### 5. Journey and Delivery Logic

- Channels in scope
- Delivery sequence
- Timing rules
- Trigger or schedule logic
- Quarantine, throttling, or priority rules

### 6. Messaging and Offer Strategy

- Core proposition
- Offer details
- CTA strategy
- Tone and personalization approach

### 7. Content and Creative Requirements

- Required modules or content blocks
- Subject line or headline direction
- Asset requirements
- Landing-page or destination requirements
- Localization or variant needs

### 8. Data and Personalization Requirements

- Required data fields
- Dynamic content logic
- Default values
- Fallback or skip conditions

### 9. Testing and QA

- Proof or UAT recipients
- Variants and test plan
- QA checkpoints
- Dependencies before launch

### 10. Risks, Dependencies, and Approvals

- Risks
- Compliance considerations
- Dependencies
- Required approvers

### 11. Assumptions and Open Questions

- Assumptions
- Open questions
